matplotlib.pyplot.legend() - Creates legend

Making a plot with log scaling:
    matplotlib.pyplot.semilogy() - does log scaling on the y-axis
    matplotlib.pyplot.semilogx() - does log scaling on the x-axis
    matplotlib.pyplot.loglog() - does log scaling on both x and y axis
    
    matplotlib.pyplot.errorbar() - plots with attached errorbars
    
    matplotlib.pyplot.bar() - plots bar chart
    
    matplotlib.pyplot.hist() - plots a histogram
    
    matplotlib.pyplot.pie() - Plots pie chart
